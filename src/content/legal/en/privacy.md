This policy describes how personal data is collected, used and protected on **josephpire.dev**. It complies with the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR, EU 2016/679)** and the **Belgian law of 30 July 2018**.

## Data controller

**Joseph Pire** — a sole proprietorship under Belgian law, based in Belgium.

- Email: [josephpire.dev@gmail.com](mailto:josephpire.dev@gmail.com)
- Belgian company number: 1036.150.733

## Data collected

### 1. Contact form (`/contact`) and project intake (`/start`)

When you submit either form, the following data is collected:

- Name
- Email address
- Message content (and, for `/start`, the answers to the scoping questions)

**Legal basis:** legitimate interest (responding to a business inquiry) and performance of pre-contractual measures at your request.

**Processing:** data is transmitted via **Web3Forms** ([web3forms.com](https://web3forms.com)) acting as data processor, then stored in the controller's mailbox.

**Retention:** 24 months from the last interaction. If the exchange leads to a contractual engagement, data is retained as required by Belgian accounting and tax obligations (7 years).

### 2. Browsing data

The site **does not deploy any tracking cookies or third-party analytics**. No cookies are set to monitor your behaviour.

The site may later integrate **Plausible Analytics** (self-hosted, cookieless, GDPR-compliant). If that happens, this section will be updated.

### 3. Site search

Search uses **[Pagefind](https://pagefind.app)**, an engine that runs entirely in your browser. Search queries are never transmitted to a server.

### 4. Server logs

The hosting provider (see [Legal notice](/en/legal)) may retain technical logs (IP addresses, timestamps, HTTP requests) for security and abuse prevention purposes, for a maximum of 30 days.

## Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights over your personal data:

- **Right of access** — know what data is held about you.
- **Right to rectification** — correct inaccurate data.
- **Right to erasure** ("right to be forgotten") — request deletion.
- **Right to data portability** — receive your data in a structured format.
- **Right to object** to processing.
- **Right to restriction of processing.**
- **Right to withdraw consent at any time.**

To exercise these rights, email [josephpire.dev@gmail.com](mailto:josephpire.dev@gmail.com). A response will be provided within one month.

## Supervisory authority

If you believe your rights are not being respected, you may file a complaint with the **Belgian Data Protection Authority (DPA)**:

- Website: [autoriteprotectiondonnees.be](https://www.autoriteprotectiondonnees.be)
- Address: Rue de la Presse 35, 1000 Brussels
