iT.SCHOOL is a SaaS platform published by Keyros SA, designed to free administrative teams in Belgian schools from time-consuming financial management tasks. Each school runs in its own hosted environment (one tenant, one URL), with dedicated interfaces for students, parents, principals, and administrative staff.

> Technical implementation details are confidential and cannot be disclosed.

## 6 Core Modules

**Users** — Complete profile per person (address, email, phone, parents, guardian, student ID) for students, teachers, and administrative staff, in compliance with GDPR.

**Groups** — Organisation of students, teachers, and classes. Some groups are created automatically (years, classes); others are created manually or imported from ProEco.

**Parent portal** — Secure interface to view spending, track attendance and lateness, order photos and textbooks, register for activities, book meals, and pay invoices.

**Finance** — Rechargeable student wallet (bank transfer or online payment); centralised accounting (revenue, client history); invoicing with overdue tracking, automatic reminders, and late fees.

**Messaging** — Email campaigns to students, teachers, and guardians with attachments; open rate and read-date tracking.

**Documents** — Downloadable invoices, report cards, and communications, customisable by student ID.

## Optional Modules

The platform also offers over ten additional modules:

- **POS (Point of Sale)** — Unlimited physical or virtual tills, sales of books, photocopies, tokens; real-time statistics and inventory
- **Meal booking** — Booking via kiosk or parent portal with immediate payment at time of reservation
- **Vending machines** — Badge-based purchases, real-time balance display, per-machine and per-product statistics
- **Attendance / Lateness** — Real-time tracking via tablet or smartphone, automatic parent notifications
- **Activities & Events** — Online registration, split payment management (school trips), automatic reminders
- **Textbook lending** — Orders by class/level, automatic invoicing and credit notes at year end
- **After-school care** — Automatic attendance recording, slot-based billing, tax certificates and ONE reports
- **Parent-teacher meetings** — Online appointment booking, email confirmation, printable schedule
- **Lockers** — Assignment, key management, automated deposit and refund
- **Photocopiers** — Direct debit from student wallet, per-machine statistics, class bulk copying
- **Class photos** — Online ordering, post-parent-approval billing, return management
